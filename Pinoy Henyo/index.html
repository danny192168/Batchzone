Info

For the rest of the players, they are revealed the word while the guesser tries to guess the word by asking others for possible hints (e.g: "Is it an animal?"). He is only responded by not-very-revealing feedbacks like "yes", "no" or "close". The guesser wins if he can guess the word within the time limit. Otherwise, he loses.


For further instructions; please, you don't have to hang the phone on someone's forhead. It's really unnecessary so you can just hold the phone if your not the guesser. Other than that, this game is not language-limited. Lastly, just enjoy the game.
